Welcome to Balloon City of Boston Inc.!

At Balloon City of Boston Inc., we take immense pride in bringing your celebrations to life with our varied range of services tailored for every occasion and budget. We specialize in custom imprinting, corporate and private event decorating, grand openings, political events, trade shows, and much more. We're dedicated to transforming your events into a spectacular experience.

Our History

Our roots are deeply embedded in East Boston / Winthrop area, where our founder, Christine Yannetty Bernstein, was born and raised. Christine's journey began in 1983 when she sold balloons at the St. Patrick's Day parade in South Boston, sparking the birth of a small retail shop, "Balloon City USA". We offered an array of balloons, gifts, and novelties. In 2002, we rebranded to Balloon City of Boston Inc., and for the past 25 years, we've been dedicated to crafting custom and unique balloon designs for every occasion.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our dedicated team of balloon professionals, including Jack, Lisa, Kelly, and Chad. Their unwavering dedication ensures your vision is brought to life beautifully, no matter the occasion.
